<p class="MsoNormal">ESTABLISHMENT OF NATIONAL ELECTIVE TAMIL LANGUAGE PROGRAMME
(NETP)</p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><b>BACKGROUND AND OBJECTIVE</b></p>

<ol type="1" start="1" style="margin-top:0in">
 <li style="mso-list:l6 level1 lfo1;tab-stops:list .5in" class="MsoNormal">The <span class="SpellE">programme</span> aims to nurture secondary school and Junior
     College (JC) students who have an aptitude in the Tamil Language to
     acquire greater proficiency in the language and deeper appreciation of the
     language, literature, and culture. The NETP <span class="SpellE">programme</span>
     will be hosted at the Umar Pulavar Tamil Language Centre (UPTLC).</li>
</ol>

<p class="MsoNormal"><b>NETP (Secondary)</b></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><i>Target Student Groups</i></p>

<ol type="1" start="1" style="margin-top:0in">
 <li style="mso-list:l4 level1 lfo2;tab-stops:list .5in" class="MsoNormal">At the
     secondary level, the NETP will be open to both Higher Tamil Language (HTL)
     students studying at UPTLC and students who offer HTL in their respective
     secondary schools and <span class="SpellE">centres</span>. Briefing sessions
     will be conducted for interested HTL students who will then register
     themselves for the NETP at UPTLC.</li>
</ol>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span class="SpellE"><i>Programme</i></span><i> Structure</i></p>

<ol type="1" start="1" style="margin-top:0in">
 <li style="mso-list:l7 level1 lfo3;tab-stops:list .5in" class="MsoNormal">For
     NETP (Secondary), HTL students could enroll in elective modules at UPTLC.
     The range of modules serves different objectives of honing their language
     skills, cultivating their appreciation of Tamil literature and culture, or
     expanding their knowledge of the Tamil history and contemporary developments
     of India. The 8 modules will be conducted on 8 Saturdays for the year. <span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp;</span></li>
 <li style="mso-list:l7 level1 lfo3;tab-stops:list .5in" class="MsoNormal">Students
     in the NETP <span class="SpellE">programme</span> will be given
     opportunities to attend cultural camps, flagship <span class="SpellE">programmes</span>
     (<span class="GramE">e.g.</span> internship <span class="SpellE">programme</span>,
     job attachment or budding writer mentoring <span class="SpellE">programme</span>),
     workshops and talks by TL experts and participate in one overseas
     immersion of at least two weeks duration to India at Secondary 3 and JC.</li>
 <li style="mso-list:l7 level1 lfo3;tab-stops:list .5in" class="MsoNormal">Overall,
     the NETP (Secondary) <span class="SpellE">programme</span> will take 100 –
     120 hours over four years from Sec 1 to Sec 4 (or about 30 hours per
     year).</li>
</ol>

     overall O-level examination results obtaining one of the following:</li>
 <ul type="disc" style="margin-top:0in">
  <li style="mso-list:l0 level2 lfo4;tab-stops:list 1.0in" class="MsoNormal">Grade
      A2 or above in Tamil; or</li>
  <li style="mso-list:l0 level2 lfo4;tab-stops:list 1.0in" class="MsoNormal">Grade
      B3 or above in Higher Tamil; or</li>
  <li style="mso-list:l0 level2 lfo4;tab-stops:list 1.0in" class="MsoNormal">Grade
      B4 or above in Higher Tamil and B3 or above in Tamil Literature; or</li>
  <li style="mso-list:l0 level2 lfo4;tab-stops:list 1.0in" class="MsoNormal">Equivalent
      results based on IP school assessment.</li>
